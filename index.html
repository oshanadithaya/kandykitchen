About Kandy Kitchen

Discover the royalty of Fine Sri Lankan Cuisine, its richness and grandeur at KandyKitchen. We have a wide range of Sri Lankan traditional meals prepared in a home-made style.

At KandyKitchen, each spice is hand selected, combined and roasted for that perfect flavour. This very act of preparing spices in right proportions makes our dishes distinct and provides you a healthy option.
